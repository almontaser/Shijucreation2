About Us

The famous Indian Silk Palace and Saree Palace are situated in the touristic hot spot of Paris, Rue du Fabourg St Denis, which is known by many as the little India of France. Not forgetting, the hub of Europe’s famous international railway station, Paris Nord, with over 100 connections throughout Europe. The establishment of Indian silk Palace was the first Indian ethnic wear gallery in France and in Europe. It was the first sign of building the ‘Little India’ area, which is today dominated by South Asian (Indian and Sri lankan) businesses of all types. Ever since, there have been numerous outlets and brands which have evolved. Shiju creation’s brand is unique in its quality and its quantity and is officially the largest warehouse for retail and whole sale Indian textile trade in Europe.

Shiju creation is the ultimate choice for some of France’s big brand names because when quality material is needed, Shiju creation never fails to produce and deliver. It is thanks to loyal customers and dedicated and amiable sales personnel who make shopping at our outlets a pleasurable and exciting experience. So we don’t want you to just feel the elegance, but be the elegant!! come and visit us now!
